about the fanlisting

glitter & sparkle dusts & magicks was created in november of 2016. mostly i was bored and missed coding. dare me has always been one of my favorite books so i wanted to make a little fanlisting of it, since none existed yet.

about the owner

hello! my name is marley. i'm 19 years old and an art student in seattle, wa. i'm hella queer and genderfluid (they/them please). i have always loved coding, my first sites were petz fansites. i made dozens of them and maybe one or two made it online (you can still see the last one here). after that i got into fanlistings for a bit but quickly abandoned those (can you see a theme here? lol). this time i decided to keep it simple so even if i got bored i wouldn't feel obligated to update except the occasional addition of a member.
